Ukrainian Alphabet Overview

The Ukrainian alphabet consists of 33 letters based on the Cyrillic script.

Vowels

Ukrainian has 10 vowel letters:

Basic Vowels (Hard)

А а /a/ like a in father

Е е /e/ like e in bed

И и /ɪ/ like i in bit

О о /o/ like o in more

У у /u/ like oo in boot

Iotated Vowels (Soft)

Я я /ja/ like ya in yard

Є є /je/ like ye in yes

І і /i/ like ee in see

Ї ї /ji/ like yi in yield

Ю ю /ju/ like yu in yule

Consonants

Stop Consonants

П п /p/ like p in park

Б б /b/ like b in book

Т т /t/ like t in top

Д д /d/ like d in dog

К к /k/ like k in kite

Г г /ɦ/ voiced h sound

Ґ ґ /g/ like g in go (rare)

Fricative Consonants

Ф ф /f/ like f in fish

В в /v/ or /w/ like v in voice

С с /s/ like s in sun

З з /z/ like z in zoo

Ш ш /ʃ/ like sh in show

Ж ж /ʒ/ like s in pleasure

Х х /x/ like ch in loch

Affricates

Ц ц /ts/ like ts in bits

Ч ч /tʃ/ like ch in church

Щ щ /ʃtʃ/ like shch

Nasals and Liquids

М м /m/ like m in man

Н н /n/ like n in no

Л л /l/ like l in love

Р р /r/ rolled r

Й й /j/ like y in yes

Special Characters

ь (soft sign) softens consonants

Apostrophe (') separates sounds

Pronunciation Tips

  • Ukrainian uses dynamic stress
  • Vowels do not reduce in unstressed positions
  • Most consonants can be hard or soft
  • Words are pronounced as spelled
